KUNG FU PANDA

Po must train a new warrior when he's chosen to become the spiritual leader of the Valley of Peace.
However, when a powerful shape-shifting sorceress sets her eyes on his Staff of Wisdom, he suddenly
realizes he's going to need some help.Teaming up with a quick-witted corsac fox, Po soon discovers
that heroes can be found in the most unexpected places.
