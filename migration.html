MIGRATION

The Mallard family is in a bit of rut. While dad Mack is content to keep his family safe paddling
mom Pam is eager to shake things up and show their kids, teen son Dax and duckling daughter Gwen,
the whole wide world around their New England pond forever.After a migrating duck family alights on
their pond with thrilling tales of far-flung places, Pam persuades Mack to embark on a family trip, via
New York City, to tropical Jamaica.

Cast

Kumail Nanjiani, Elizabeth Banks, Awkwafina, Keegan-Michael Key, Danny DeVito

Directed by
